Transformative Education
Diagnostic Tool

This self-assessment tool is designed to help educators in health professions education reflect on their teaching practices across eight principles of transformative education. It is not a performance measure — it is a compass for professional growth.

1

Complete all 8 principles

Each principle has 5 statements. Rate how often each describes your practice. Use N/A if a statement does not apply to your context.

2

Be honest and reflective

There are no right or wrong answers. The tool is most useful when you respond based on your current practice, not your aspirations.

3

See your results instantly

A radar diagram and colour-coded scores appear live as you answer. Each principle is scored 0–25.

4

Interpret and act

Use the CAN-DO statements and teaching activity examples to identify where you are and where you want to go next in your development.
